1. Introduction

Metrixy Tools is a multi-functional utility application developed by DR.IT.Studio. The app includes features such as Notes, Calculator, Unit Converter, and Fuel Calculator.

This Privacy Policy explains how data is handled when using the application, including information related to advertising and paid subscriptions.

Metrixy Tools is distributed via Huawei AppGallery. All advertising, subscriptions, and data processing practices comply with Huawei AppGallery, Huawei Ads, GDPR, and applicable privacy regulations.

Important: By downloading, installing, or using the Metrixy Tools app, you confirm that you have read, understood, and agreed to this Privacy Policy.

2. Data Collection Overview

Metrixy Tools does not independently collect, store, or process personal data during normal use of the application.

Certain device-related and advertising data may be collected only when advertisements are displayed, and only by third-party advertising services.

Advertising is enabled by default in the free (Lite) version of the app and can be completely disabled only by purchasing the Pro subscription.

3. Information We Do NOT Collect

The Metrixy Tools application does not collect:

  • Contacts
  • Phone numbers
  • Messages
  • Photos or media files
  • Camera or microphone data
  • Precise location data
  • Background activity data
  • Data from other applications

4. User-Provided Information

Users may voluntarily provide limited information, such as:

  • Email address when contacting customer support
  • Content manually entered into the app (notes, calculations, fuel records, unit conversions)

This information:

  • Is stored locally on the device
  • Is not transmitted to our servers
  • Is not shared with third parties for marketing or profiling purposes

5. Advertising and Third-Party Data Collection

5.1 Advertising Model

The free version of Metrixy Tools (Lite) displays advertisements to support development and maintenance of the app.

Advertising behavior in the Lite version:

  • Banner ads may appear on certain screens
  • Interstitial ads may appear between screens
  • Rewarded video ads are offered optionally to unlock temporary premium features

The Pro subscription completely removes all advertising, including banner, interstitial, and rewarded ads.

5.2 Advertising Services

Metrixy Tools uses the Huawei Ads SDK to display advertisements.

When advertisements are shown, Huawei Ads may automatically collect certain advertising-related data, such as:

  • Huawei Open Advertising Identifier (OAID)
  • Device type, model, and operating system version
  • Approximate location (country or region level only)
  • Ad interaction data (impressions, clicks, rewards)

This data is collected solely for advertising purposes, including:

  • Ad delivery
  • Ad performance measurement
  • Fraud prevention
  • Rewarded advertising functionality

The Metrixy Tools application itself does not access, store, or process this data directly.

5.3 Advertising Identifier (OAID)

The Huawei Ads SDK may collect the Huawei Open Advertising Identifier (OAID).

OAID is used exclusively for:

  • Displaying advertisements
  • Measuring advertising performance
  • Preventing fraud and abuse

Users can reset or limit the advertising identifier in device settings: Settings ‚Üí Privacy ‚Üí Ads

5.4 Rewarded Advertising

Rewarded video ads are provided under the following conditions:

  • Watching rewarded ads is always optional
  • Users are clearly informed about the reward before watching
  • Rewards are granted only after the ad is watched in full
  • The duration and scope of the reward are clearly stated
  • Rewarded ads are never mandatory for using core app features

6. Permissions and Device Access

Metrixy Tools requests only the minimum permissions required for proper operation:

Permission Purpose
INTERNET Loading advertisements and optional online features
ACCESS_NETWORK_STATE Checking network availability
ACCESS_WIFI_STATE Optimizing ad delivery

The app does not request or use permissions related to:

  • Camera
  • Microphone
  • Contacts
  • Location
  • Files or storage access

7. Paid Features and Subscriptions

7.1 Pro Subscription

The Pro subscription provides:

  • Complete removal of all advertisements
  • Access to premium themes
  • Access to all premium features
  • Priority support

7.2 Payment Processing

All payments are processed securely via Huawei In-App Purchases.

  • We do not store or process payment card data
  • All billing information is handled by Huawei
  • Subscriptions auto-renew unless cancelled
  • Subscription management is available via Huawei AppGallery settings

8. Data Storage and Security

8.1 Local Storage

All user-generated content is stored locally on the device.

  • We do not have access to local user data
  • Data is removed when the app is uninstalled or data is cleared

Cloud synchronization is not enabled by default and is not used unless explicitly introduced in future versions.

8.2 Security Measures

  • The app does not transmit personal data without explicit user action
  • All communications with advertising services use encrypted connections (HTTPS)
  • Users are encouraged to use device security features (PIN, biometrics, screen lock)

8.3 Data Retention

  • Local data is retained until deleted by the user
  • Support correspondence is retained for up to 2 years
  • Advertising performance data retention is governed by Huawei Ads policies

9. User Control and Choices

Users can control their data by:

  • Clearing app data via system settings
  • Revoking permissions in Android settings
  • Disabling personalized ads in device settings
  • Resetting the advertising identifier (OAID)
  • Removing all ads by purchasing the Pro subscription
  • Requesting data deletion via support email

10. Children's Privacy

Metrixy Tools is not intended for children under the age of 13.

We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If such data is identified, it will be deleted immediately upon verification.

11. GDPR and International Users

Users located in the European Union have rights under the GDPR, including:

  • Right to restrict processing
  • Right to data portability
  • Right to object
  • Right to withdraw consent
