1. Introduction

The Metrixy app, developed by DR.IT.Studio ("we", "us", "our"), is a multi-functional utility application featuring Notes, Calculator, Unit Converter, and Fuel Calculator.

This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, how we use, store, protect, and transfer it, including information about advertising and paid subscriptions.

The app is distributed via Huawei AppGallery, and all advertising and subscription features comply with Huawei requirements.

Important: By downloading, installing, or using the Metrixy app, you agree to this Privacy Policy.

2. What Data We Collect

2.1 Personal Information

We do not collect personal data automatically. The user may voluntarily provide:

  • email address when contacting support;
  • content entered manually in the app (notes, calculations, fuel records, unit conversions).

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties for marketing purposes.

2.2 Non-Personal (Technical) Information

For diagnostics, service improvement, and advertising, we may collect anonymous data such as:

  • device type, model, and manufacturer;
  • operating system version;
  • interface language and region settings;
  • frequency and method of using app features;
  • error data (crash logs);
  • device advertising identifier (OAID for Huawei devices).

2.3 Advertising Identifier (OAID)

We use the Huawei Open Advertising Identifier (OAID) exclusively for:

  • displaying relevant advertisements;
  • measuring ad performance;
  • fraud prevention.

You can reset or disable OAID in your device settings under Privacy > Ads.

4. Advertising and Third-Party Services

4.1 Advertising Model

The free version of Metrixy (Lite) displays advertisements to support development and maintenance of the app.

Advertising is always displayed in the Lite version:

  • Banner ads appear at the bottom of the screen;
  • Interstitial ads may appear between screens;
  • Rewarded video ads are available optionally for temporary premium features.

Pro subscribers do not see any advertisements. Purchasing a Pro subscription completely removes all advertising from the app.

4.2 Advertising Network

We use Huawei Ads SDK to display advertisements. Huawei Ads may display:

  • personalized ads (based on your interests and device data);
  • non-personalized ads (based on context only).

You can choose your ad preference in the app settings or device settings.

4.3 Rewarded Advertising (Rewarded Video)

  • Users can voluntarily watch video ads to temporarily unlock premium features;
  • Watching rewarded ads is always optional ‚Äî never mandatory;
  • Before showing the ad, users receive a clear explanation of the feature they will unlock;
  • The reward is provided only after the ad is watched in full;
  • Premium access duration is clearly stated before watching.

4.4 Technologies Used by Advertising Services

Huawei Ads may use:

  • advertising identifiers (OAID);
  • cookies or similar technologies;
  • aggregated anonymous data for ad targeting.

Third-Party Policies:

5.1 Pro Subscription

The Pro subscription provides:

  • Complete ad removal ‚Äî no banners, interstitials, or rewarded video prompts;
  • Access to all premium themes;
  • Access to all premium features;
  • Priority support.

5.2 Payment Processing

All payments are processed securely via Huawei In-App Purchases.

  • We do not store or process payment card data;
  • All payment information is handled by Huawei;
  • Subscriptions auto-renew unless cancelled;
  • You can manage subscriptions in Huawei AppGallery settings.

6. Control Over Your Data

You have full control over your data:

  • Delete app data: Clear all saved data in app settings or Android Settings > Apps > Metrixy > Clear Data;
  • Revoke permissions: Manage permissions in Android Settings > Apps > Metrixy > Permissions;
  • Disable personalized ads: Settings > Privacy > Ads on your device;
  • Remove all ads: Purchase Pro subscription;
  • Reset advertising ID: Settings > Privacy > Ads > Reset Advertising ID;
  • Request data deletion: Contact support@dr-it.studio or use Data Deletion Form

7.3 Data Retention
