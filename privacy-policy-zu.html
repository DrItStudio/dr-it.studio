1. Isingeniso

Forest Calculator wuhlelo lokusebenza olwakhiwe yi-DR.IT.Studio ukuze lwenze izibalo zobuningi bezinkuni nezinye izici zobuchwepheshe. Le Nqubomgomo Yobumfihlo ichaza ukuthi yiziphi izindlela esiqoqa ngazo idatha, ukuthi siyisebenzisa kanjani, siyigcina kanjani, siyivikela futhi siyabelana ngayo — kufaka phakathi nezikhangiso ezibukwayo ezinikeza imivuzo (rewarded video ads).

2. Idatha Esiyiqoqayo

2.1 Ulwazi Lomuntu Siqu

Asiqoqi ulwazi lomuntu siqu ngokuzenzakalela. Nokho, umsebenzisi anganikeza:

  • Ikheli le-imeyili lapho ethintana nethimba lokusekela;
  • Okuqukethwe okungeniswe ngesandla kuhlelo lokusebenza (isb. izibalo).

2.2 Ulwazi Olungeyona Lwomuntu Siqu (Lobuchwepheshe)

Ukuze senze ukuthuthuka, silungise izinkinga futhi sikhombise izikhangiso:

  • Uhlobo lwedivayisi kanye nenguqulo ye-OS;
  • Ulimi lohlelo lokusebenza;
  • Imvamisa nokusetshenziswa;
  • Ama-crash logs;
  • I-Advertising ID.

4. Izikhangiso Namasevisi Esithathu

4.1 Ukwaziswa Okuvamile

Izikhangiso zingavela kubalingani abanjengo-Google AdMob. Ungakhetha uhlobo lwezikhangiso nasesikhathini esizayo.

4.2 Izikhangiso Zevidiyo Zemivuzo

Lolu hlelo lunikeza izikhangiso zevidiyo aziphoqelekile. Ungakhetha ukuzibuka ukuze uthole amanye amathuba.

Qaphela:

  • Ukubuka izikhangiso kuyikhethelo lakho;
  • Sizokuchazela ngaphambi kokudlala ukuthi yini oyizothola;
  • Umvuzo uyonikwa kuphela uma ugcine ubuke yonke ividiyo;
  • Asabelani ngemininingwane yomuntu siqu nabalingani bezikhangiso.

4.3 Ubuchwepheshe Esibunawo

Abalingani (njengo-Google) bangasebenzisa:

  • Imininingwane yezikhangiso;
  • Ama-cookie noma okunye okulingana nakho;
  • Idatha egxilile kuzikhangiso.

Inqubomgomo ye-Google: https://policies.google.com/technologies/ads

Zonke izinkokhelo zenziwa ngo-Google Play. Asigcini imininingwane yephakethe lakho.
