Forest Calculator - Privacy Policy

How we protect your data and ensure your privacy
Home > Forest Calculator > Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 13, 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how DR.IT.Studio ("we," "our," or "us") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use the Forest Calculator mobile application (the "App").

🔒 Privacy-First Approach

Forest Calculator is designed with privacy as a core principle. The app operates completely offline and does not collect, transmit, or store any personal data on external servers.

1. Information We DO NOT Collect

Forest Calculator is designed to respect your privacy. We do not collect:

  • Personal information (name, email, phone number)
  • Location data or GPS coordinates
  • Usage analytics or tracking data
  • Device information beyond basic system requirements
  • Calculation results or measurement data
  • Photos or files you process within the app
  • Any data that could identify you personally

2. Local Data Storage

All app functionality operates locally on your device:

  • Calculation History: Stored locally on your device and never transmitted
  • App Settings: Language preferences and settings remain on your device
  • Custom Configurations: Your personal calculation setups stay private
  • Export Files: PDF, Excel, and CSV files are generated locally

You have full control over this data and can delete it at any time through your device's app management settings.

3. Offline Operation

Forest Calculator functions completely offline:

  • No internet connection required for any functionality
  • All calculations performed locally on your device
  • Wood species database stored locally
  • Export functions work without internet access
  • No data synchronization with external servers

4. Third-Party Services

Forest Calculator may include optional third-party services:

4.1 Advertisement Services (Optional)

If you choose to watch reward videos to unlock premium features:

  • Ads are served by third-party providers (Google AdMob)
  • These services may collect limited device information for ad delivery
  • No personal calculation data is shared with advertisers
  • You can avoid ads entirely by purchasing the PRO version

4.2 In-App Purchases

Payment processing is handled by your device's app store (Google Play, App Store):

  • We do not store payment information
  • Purchase history is managed by the app store
  • Transactions are processed according to app store privacy policies

5. Data Security

We implement security measures to protect your privacy:

  • No cloud storage or external data transmission
  • Local encryption for sensitive app data
  • Regular security updates through app store releases
  • Minimal permissions requested from your device

6. Children's Privacy

Forest Calculator does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. Since we don't collect personal data from any users, the app is inherently safe for all age groups. Parents should supervise app store purchases if allowing children to use the app.

7. Your Rights and Controls

You have complete control over your data:

  • Data Deletion: Uninstall the app to remove all local data
  • Settings Reset: Clear app data through device settings
  • Export Control: Choose what data to include in exported files
  • Ad-Free Experience: Purchase PRO version to avoid optional ads

8. International Users

Forest Calculator is used globally. Since no personal data is collected or transmitted:

  • No cross-border data transfers occur
  • Local privacy laws are respected automatically
  • GDPR, CCPA, and other privacy regulations are inherently complied with
  • No data residency concerns

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do:

  • The updated date will be revised at the top of this policy
  • Significant changes will be announced through app store release notes
  • Continued use of the app constitutes acceptance of the updated policy
  • We will maintain our commitment to minimal data collection

Contact Information

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or our privacy practices:

Email: support@dr-it.studio

We will respond to privacy-related inquiries within 48 hours.

🌲 Forest Calculator Promise

Your measurement data, calculations, and work remain completely private. Forest Calculator is designed for professional foresters who need reliable, offline tools without privacy concerns. Your forest data stays in the forest – where it belongs.
